Operating Philosophy

01

Support is Reliability Engineering

Tier 3 isn't where tickets go to die — it's where systemic problems get eliminated permanently. Every recurring incident is a failure to engineer prevention.

02

Measure What Matters

If you can't measure it, you can't improve it. Build KPIs that drive behavior — not vanity metrics that justify headcount.

03

Automate the Repeatable

Every manual process is technical debt. Invest in automation that uplifts the entire organization — not just your team.

04

Global Scale, Local Accountability

Build delivery models that follow the sun without losing ownership. Every engineer owns their outcomes regardless of timezone.
